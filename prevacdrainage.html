Needle-Free Closed-Wound Drainage Systems

Our "Latex Free", plastic Pre-Vac bottles offer the convenience of glass evacuation bottles without the safety issues of glass. The Pre-Vac bottles are a closed system that won't break and they utilize a needle-less technique for added safety.

We stock 600ml and 1000ml sizes that can be ordered sterile or non-sterile, with or without tube set. The sterile bottles with tube set provide nurse efficiencies in set up, use and disposal. And our unique 120cm drain line with roller clamp allows the control of drainage fluid flow to minimize any discomfort a patient might experience.

Features & Benefits

  • The ready-charged high-vacuum state of the bottles makes them easy to use just by attaching the wound drainage lines.
  • A Luer lock further protects the users from fluid contamination.
  • The manometric bellow indicates the amount of negative pressure present in the bottle, making the vacuum easy to read.
  • The calibration indicated on the clear wall of the bottle is to facilitate the measurement of both quantity and quality of fluid and exudates.
