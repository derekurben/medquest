NextGen CCM

Chronic Care Management Chronic Care Management (CCM) is a new 2015 Medicare program designed to support patient wellness and avoid worsening conditions. Defined as the non-face-to-face services provided to Medicare beneficiaries who have two or more significant chronic conditions, CCM requires complex, specific duties and documentation that create challenges for even the savviest practices.

Fortunately, NextGen WellCare CCM offers a solution so that physicians can concentrate on caring for their patients. Our NextGen WellCare CCM program provides a secure platform focused on identifying patients that meet the criteria for CCM and then works directly with your patients to create a Care Plan unique to each patient.

Each patientsâ€™ Care Plan meets all the requirements of the 20-minute non-face-to-face monthly service, coordinates care among all providers and allows them full records access. NextGen WellCare CCM benefits the patient with monthly assistance and provides recommendations as it relates to their health.

Our program is tailored to each physicians needs, including optional billing, claims submission, denial management and follow up. Contact us to hear all the details of how NextGen WellCare CCM can help your patients, save you time and increase practice revenues.

NextGen WellCheck

NextGen Wellcheck - Website

The Annual Wellness Visit (AWV) is the first health-screening program ever implemented by Medicare and free for all Medicare Part B and Advantage patients. Unfortunately, only 15% of eligible patients are receiving the AWV due to the time it takes for completion and the complex guidelines/requirements that must be met for full reimbursement.

Our WellCheck program takes all the required extra work away from the physician and staff so they can focus on patient care.

Benefits to Providers

  • 70-80% patient capture rate vs. 15% national average

  • Engages both active and inactive Medicare patients

  • No change in physician workflow

  • Compliant with 100% of clinical value indicators

  • No upfront costs or long-term commitments

  • Generates significant new revenue to the practice

Benefits to Patients

  • Improves patient quality of life

  • Strong preventive measures to support patient wellness and avoid chronic illness issues

  • Creates a 5-year Health Plan specific to the patient to help maintain their wellness and focus on prevention instead of treatment

  • No Cost to the patient (zero deductible)
