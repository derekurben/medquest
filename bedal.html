The Product


The newly patented and FDA registered BedalPatch Comfort allows patients to safely shower during IV treatments. Unlike current solutions, the BedalPatch Comfort is specifically designed for patients to shower while connected or disconnected from their IV line, keeping the catheter exit site dry and minimizing the risk of infection. According to CDC statistics, approximately 12% to 25% of BSI (bloodstream infections) associated with IV therapy are fatal. The CDC suggest that these infections can be minimized if proper care is taken to keep the catheter site clean and dry from moisture.


The Story


The BedalPatch was developed by David De Munter a cancer patient that lived through the challenges of showering with an indwelling catheter. Knowing that BSI infections can be fatal and the importance of keeping his exit site clean and dry, David was determined to find a way that would allow patients the freedom of a stress free daily shower. David’s background in architecture led him to design what is now the first product that specifically allows patients to shower with or without their IV line connected.
