Needle-Free Closed-Wound Drainage Systems

Our "Latex Free", plastic Pre-Vac bottles offer the convenience of glass evacuation bottles without the safety issues of glass. The Pre-Vac bottles are a closed system that won't break and they utilize a needle-less technique for added safety.

We stock 600ml and 1000ml sizes that can be ordered sterile or non-sterile, with or without tube set. The sterile bottles with tube set provide nurse efficiencies in set up, use and disposal. And our unique 120cm drain line with roller clamp allows the control of drainage fluid flow to minimize any discomfort a patient might experience.

Features & Benefits

  • The ready-charged high-vacuum state of the bottles makes them easy to use just by attaching the wound drainage lines.
  • A Luer lock further protects the users from fluid contamination.
  • The manometric bellow indicates the amount of negative pressure present in the bottle, making the vacuum easy to read.
  • The calibration indicated on the clear wall of the bottle is to facilitate the measurement of both quantity and quality of fluid and exudates.

Affordable Fecal Collection & Closed Catheter Systems

MedQuest USA, LLC. of Elgin, Illinois, sells affordable fecal collection and closed catheter systems. Fecal incontinence is a distressing problem for the patient and a priority for health care professionals who need to balance maintaining patient dignity, risk of cross infection, and prevention of skin damage. We have the right systems to help you get the job done.

Fecal Collection System

This latex-free closed catheter system is ideal for management of feces incontinence for patients with little or no bowel control, and can be used for up to 29 consecutive days. It is a temporary fecal containment device developed to manage bedridden, immobilized, and incontinent patients as an alternative to traditional methods such as pads, diapers, fecal pouches, and rectal tubes.

It can also be used for diverting feces from the burned area to diminish the risk of skin breakdown and avoid serious infections. It also protects the patient's wounds from feces to help prevent cross infection and preserve skin integrity.

Features & Benefits

  • This catheter allows the feces to drain into an external collection bag to eliminate the need for excessive linen changes and patient cleansing.
  • It reduces the risk of skin breakdown due to inflammation of the perineum skins.
  • It minimizes exposure to infectious microorganisms.
  • The soft, low-pressure inflatable balloon conforms to patient anatomy, ensuring optimum seating to aid easy insertion and removal, and to minimize the chance of tissue necrosis.
  • Save time and money associated with bed linen changes and cleanups to improve nursing efficiency.
  • The blue "IRRIG" port enables immediate identification of the catheter irrigation port to help eliminate accidental inflation.
  • The syringe is precisely labeled to 45ml to reduce any chance of over-inflation and related patient discomfort.
  • The odor-resistant collection bag assures quick, safe disposal of waste.
