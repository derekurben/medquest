About Our Manufacturer


Founded in 1977, Pacific Hospital Supply Co. Ltd (PAHSCO) specializes in providing hospitals with disposable medical supplies. PAHSCO employs its own research-and-development team and technical engineers, and they operate their own production lines in order to ensure the superior quality that has become synonymous with the PAHSCO brand.

As evidence, their facilities have obtained GMP, ISO 13485 certification, and the majority of their products have received CE certification or UL approval. Today, they have become an industry leader and a trusted partner to their health care industry clients while exporting more than 600 different products to more than 80 countries and regions.

To remain at the top of the industry, PAHSCO has continually directed substantial resources to the research and development of products as well as constantly refined their manufacturing process through open communication channels with their clients. They commit themselves to facilitating their health care providers with safer, more efficient, and cost-effective products. Caregivers worldwide look to them for their health care solutions.
