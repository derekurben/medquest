Universal Drainage Kits For Home Use

Our Universal Drainage Kits connect to any in-dwelling catheter. Our kits contain all necessary items needed for a safe and affordable drainage at home, eliminating travel to your provider.


Kit Contents:

      Package 1: Sterile 1000ml Pre-Vac Drainage Bottle with Connecting Tube Set


      Package 2: Sterile Procedure Kit – Includes the following;


    • 1 ea. Tegaderm Dressing, 1628
    • 1 ea. Foam Pad with 2” Split
    • 8 ea. Alcohol Wipes
    • 1 ea. Tube Pinch Clamp
    • 12 ea. Gauze 4”x4”
    • 1 ea. Vented Luer Cap
    • 2 pair Chemo Rated Nitrile Gloves
    • 1 ea. No Sting Barrier Film


      • Package 3: Safety Drain Line Adaptor for non-luer lock Catheters


      Package 4: Sterile Cap for non-luer lock Catheters
